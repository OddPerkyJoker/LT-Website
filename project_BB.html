The Brief

How can we make people see the importance of Sustainability in packaging and product design?

Using research as a starting point, create a new branding style for the eco-friendly based dog grooming company.

A luxury pet grooming product brand with a focus on sustainability and reusability.

Working with Betty and Bruno, we were tasked with developing a brand identity centered around, sustainability with an emphasis on eco-friendly products and materials.
